If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. Henceforth, you are strongly encouraged to find out whether the case can be heard at the ECT, and complete the pre-filing assessment, via the Community Justice and Tribunals system. before proceeding to file a claim. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
