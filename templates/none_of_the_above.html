If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter.

Henceforth, you are strongly encouraged to visit the Judiciary website to understand the process of filing an employment claim, which include the following:

1. Complete the pre-filing assessment via the Community Justice and Tribunals system. You will receive a pre-filing assessment ID after completing the pre-filing assessment;

2. File your claim on the Community Justice and Tribunals System (CJTS);

3. Serve the documents on the respondent within 7 working days of the date of filing of the claim; and

4. File a declaration of service (DOS) within 4 weeks of the date of filing of the claim or before the date of the first case management conference, whichever is earlier.
