Appeal against an Employment Claims Order

A party may appeal against a decision made at a case management conference by the registrar or against the decision of a tribunal at a hearing.

For more information, please visit the Judiciary website.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity (including private limited), you may proceed to obtain a queue number for the one-off free 20-minute legal advice session, from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts.
