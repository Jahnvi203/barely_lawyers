If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. Henceforth, you are strongly encouraged to obtain details of the employment claim filed against you, via the Community Justice and Tribunals system. You should also find out whether the case can be heard at the ECT, before responding to the claim. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
