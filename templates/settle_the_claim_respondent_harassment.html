Your case is under Simplified Proceedings. If you do not dispute the claim, you may resolve it by initiating an online negotiation on the Community Justice & Tribunal System (CJTS).

If both parties manage to reach a settlement before the date of the Case Management Conference (CMC) or Pre-Trial Conference (PTC), the claimant may apply to withdraw the claim. Alternatively, both parties may apply for a consent order to be issued.

If the dispute is not settled by the date of the CMC or PTC as stated in the Notice of Pre-Trial or Case Conference served on you, you will have to attend the case conference or PTC.

For more information, please visit the Judiciary website.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity (including private limited), you may proceed to obtain a queue number for the one-off free 20-minute legal advice session, from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts.
