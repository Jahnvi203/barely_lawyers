If you believe that a criminal offence has been committed against you, you should make a police report and check if your case is eligible, before filing a Magistrate's Complaint online to the State Courts. You may go through an online assessment to give you a preliminary assessment of whether your case is suitable for filing as a Magistrate's Complaint. Please click here for more details on Magistrate's Complaints.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. You may obtain a queue number from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts, which are issued on a first-come, first-served basis.
