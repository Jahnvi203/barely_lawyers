If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. You may appeal against a decision made at a case management conference or pre-trial conference by the deputy registrar or against the decision of a judge in the Protection from Harassment Court. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
