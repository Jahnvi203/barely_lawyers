Settle the Claim

If you do not dispute the claim, you may resolve it by initiating an online negotiation on the Community Justice & Tribunal System (CJTS)

If the dispute is not settled by the date of the pre-trial conference (PTC) or case conference as stated in the Notice of Pre-Trial or Case Conference served on you, you will have to attend the PTC or case conference.

For more information, please visit the Judiciary website.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity (including private limited), you may proceed to obtain a queue number for the one-off free 20-minute legal advice session, from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts.
