If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter.

Henceforth, you are strongly encouraged to visit the Judiciary website to understand the process of filing a neighbour dispute claim which includes the following:

1. Complete the pre-filing assessment on the Community Justice and Tribunals system and take note of the pre-filing assessment ID after completing the pre-filing assessment;

2. Prepare proof or evidence in support of your neighbour dispute claim;

3. File your claim via the Community Justice and Tribunals System (CJTS) within 2 years of the event which creates your cause of action;

4. Serve the documents on the respondent within 14 days of the date of filing of the claim; and

5. File a declaration of service (DOS) before the date of the first pre-trial conference or case conference.
