Settle the Claim

If you do not dispute the claim, you may resolve it by initiating an online negotiation or mediation on the Community Justice & Tribunal System (CJTS)

If a settlement is not reached, or if an application for a consent order is not approved by the Employment Claims Tribunal, you will have to attend the case management conference as stated in the Notice of CMC.

For more information, please visit the Judiciary website.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity (including private limited), you may proceed to obtain a queue number for the one-off free 20-minute legal advice session, from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts.
