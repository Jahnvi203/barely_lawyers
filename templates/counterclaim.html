File a Counterclaim

If you also have an eligible claim against the claimant, you may file a counterclaim against the claimant in the same claim. You will need to take a pre-filing assessment and serve the claim on the other party.

• File at least 3 days before the scheduled consultation
• Take a pre-filing assessment and serve the claim on the other party

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. Henceforth, you are strongly encouraged to complete the pre-filing assessment via the Community Justice and Tribunals system for a preliminary indication if your matter falls under their purview. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
