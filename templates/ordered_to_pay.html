The Protection from Harassment Court (PHC) hears matters related to harassment, stalking, cyberbullying and other acts causing alarm or distress. Please click here for more details and here for the type of cases the PHC can hear. You may also take an online pre-filing assessment on the Community Justice and Tribunals System (CJTS) to consider if you have a claim and whether it is suitable for the simplified proceedings in the PHC.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. You may obtain a queue number from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts, which are issued on a first-come, first-served basis.
