If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. A party may file an appeal against a decision made at a pre-trial conference or case conference by the registrar, to a tribunal magistrate, or, appeal against a decision made at a hearing by a tribunal to the General Division of the High Court under specific circumstances. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
