Appeal against a Neighbour Dispute Claim

A party may appeal against a decision made at a pre-trial conference or case conference by the registrar or against a decision made at a hearing by a tribunal to the General Division of the High Court under specific circumstances.

For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.

If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity (including private limited), you may proceed to obtain a queue number for the one-off free 20-minute legal advice session, from the ticket-issuing machine at Level 1, Information Counter of the State Courts.
