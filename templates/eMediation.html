If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. Either party to the case may request eMediation if the parties are unable to reach a settlement through eNegotiation. Henceforth, it is strongly encouraged that parties proceed with eMediation via the Community Justice and Tribunals system. For more information, you may refer to the Judiciary website.
