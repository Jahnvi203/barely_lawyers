If you are not enquiring on behalf of a company or business entity, (including private limited), please note that the On-Site Legal Advice Scheme entitles you to a one-off free legal consultation on the subject-matter. A default order may be made against you if you are absent for a Protection from Harassment Court (PHC) court session. If you wish to apply to set aside a default order made in your absence, please refer to the Judiciary website.
